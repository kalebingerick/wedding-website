Our Story

We met...sometime at the beginning of college. In 2014, we both decided to give Greek life a try, and spent the next few years bumping into each other at the occasional event and hanging out in the fraternity lounge. The details are a bit hazy, but this story really begins in 2016 - our senior year.

We were "Parent Trapped". Our friends set us up for each other like dominoes, and those dominoes came crashing down with one late night text from Kaleb after those friends left us talking together at a sorority formal. “You coming to the potluck on Sunday?”

What happens next is the montage of all of those cute firsts. Cute weekend adventures, cute dates, cute nights spent talking and cracking jokes together until we were both so tired that everything seemed funny. The first time we said ‘I love you,’ the first time meeting each other’s friends and families, the first time we both cried together. We helped each other through one of the most exciting but terrifying times of our lives and by the time graduation started looming, we knew we were in it for the long haul.

Kaleb secured a job offer in Boston early on in the semester and with a lot of Googling, emailing, and interviewing, it worked out pretty dang well that Sarah secured her dream job in the same city. “Convenient”, right?

Moving to Boston together was a dream realized. It became our first home together. We made our friends and eventually moved into our apartment. Shortly after, we decided to adopt our dog Phoebe who, as many of you know, is our sassy little pride and joy.

Kaleb proposed in October 2021 after a year of hiding the ring in his sock drawer waiting for the right moment. Spoiler alert, she said yes.

In 2022, rather than planning a wedding, we decided to fulfill another dream first and bought our house here in Rochester. Moving away from Boston wasn’t easy, but we couldn’t be happier with the decision to lay down roots. We adopted our second sassy (slightly bigger) pride and joy, Fergus, and have been spending our days learning how to DIY, exploring the city, training the pups, and making 2016 Kaleb and Sarah proud.
